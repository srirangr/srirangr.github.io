For Deepti 
Meet G P Deepti, the girl who has it all. One hell of a girl she is. I am yet to find a girl as witty as her. Her dedication and zeal towards her work can inspire laziest of the being. She is a chatterbox and always full of fun. Loves reading books and has a great taste for music. She is almost always involved in more than one work, and that means she is well too good at multitasking. She has a lovely heart which cannot see her friends in distress and would do whatever it takes to put a smile on their faces. And it doesnt stop here, her initiative to work for a social cause which gave birth to Samvedana is, I am sure, going to be a milestone in her life and will touch (up) many others. She involves her heart and soul in whatever she does. She likes to be tough on the outside, but she actually is very gentle from inside. She says that she is not sentimental but the ones close to her wont agree with it. This is about the girl I know best in IIIT and I cant help praising her for her awesomeness.
Now lets go back in time and see how we met. It was second or third semester, I recall we met in front of GH. I dont know how we met but I am glad we did. We had hardly talked to each other and even when we did we were in groups. She talked to me very freely and I listened to her intently. I think I gave her some advice, at which I must confess I am not very good, and I hope she found it useful. From then on we have tried to retain our friendship through the thick and things that we have been through. Sometimes she couldnt help and sometimes I screwed it up very badly. And now I am so glad that we managed to get through all of it. I still remember the feeling when she gave me a coffee cup on my birthday. I love coffee cups and I love birthday presents. Apparently I had mentioned to her that I like coffee cups and she remembered it. That was very sweet of you. I did or didnt do a few things that I shouldn't or should have done. I am still amazed how could she forgive me for all those stupidity so easily.

For Prashant Singh

The other one, well we will talk about him in his testi . In first meeting, you cant help but notice the clarity with which he utters every word. Everything he says are measured and moulded by his strong thoughts and experiences engraved in the pages of his life.
If its been fantastic four years, its mostly because of his friendship with me. He has been there to listen to my thoughts, to take away my sorrows, to support me in moments of doubts. I remember the treat that he gave me in canteen because he heard that I got max marks in some subject, which I didnt get of course . He was happy for me and I was happy that I have such a friend. Thats the best treat I have had and no other can come even close to it.
I cant recount here the various discussions we have had on the topics ranging from personal matters to philosophical and from political to social. One thing that defines him is his strong belief in whatever he believes. He is an avid reader. The thoughts that he has extracted from his readings and his experiences shaped him into what he is today. He doesnt get affected by someone's views easily. You have to covince him and even then he would have a doubt in a corner of his mind. He will think it over and over and test it before he is actually convinced of the idea. Thats how strong his character is.
He holds a very special place for his close friends and would go to any lengths to make sure that his friends are happy and safe. And the feelings are reciprocated by his friends. If you ask his friends about him they wont stop praising him and then they will tell you about his gluttony . He manages to impress anyone in the first meeting itself.
