Sanket 'Market' Sharma alias Daddu, is the most talked about guy of our batch. Bring anything up on the discussion table, his name is sure to crop up. We've shared relationship has been very eccentric all these years. More of a blow hot - blow cold type. I remember him saying one thing to me - 'When our college days would become numbered, no matter how many arguments there might have been between us, all of these would be forgotten in those times of nostalgia'. I must say you were very correct that day . I look back at those days and laugh at the arguments we've had. It all seems so laughable now.

He is someone who've I've admired for his managerial skills. No event is possible in IIIT without him being a part of it. Felicity, Placements, Batch trips etc. he's done them all. . Also his commitment during placements has been really worth appreciating. Even after getting placed, he took a number of tutorials to teach many of us. The concern he showed about me getting placed was really heart-warming.

He is one of the very very few guys of our batch who can build a great career in politics if he wishes to (Give this option a serious thought, offer letter from Bank of America is still pending ). On that note, I would like to advise you to be a little less politically correct. Speak you heart out and you'll do just as great. Being measured and calculated always doesn't help. Let your heart speak for you .

Well, it has truly been wonderful being your friend knowing that there would always be a helping hand from your side. A guy who knows how to handle different types of people . It is a great quality dude, one that I have always longed to learn but have not quite done it.

Here's wishing you all the very best for the future (I know you'll be pretty successful in what ever you do).

Cheers
