On the first day of college, I saw a bit blackish and plumpy guy and I wished, Oh God! Please don't let me sit beside him. But my wish never came true. This guy was my neighbour(in class as well as in hostel) and the only neighbour I had. To add icing on the cake he was also in A4, he was also my PCmate in workspace till the end of first year. So we HAD to attend all the tutorials and English classes together. God only knows how I tolerated him.

Believe it or not, this guy slept in the very first class held in IIIT. And I was not able to control my laugh seeing his face . Eyes closed, his chin on his palm, face as oily as if the last time he washed his face was during stone age, body odour of like a shepherd and saliva drooling from his lips.. yuck!! Looking at his inncoent face, Madhava Krishna once asked him "Do you even belong to this class?". And we were all ROFL .

Thats how his appereance is. Takes bath once in a month, never combes his hair, always comes to class with a cloudy (mabbu) face. That is the reason why he has been awarded the title of "Cloud Star" .

But looks are deceptive. He's a very talented guy, who listens to classes even while asleep, who plays Age and Dota awesomely, who is an awesome coder. Also he's very dedicated to his work. I came to know about his hidden talent when we were doing BTP under Prof. Rammurthy. Unlike my BTP partner, he helped me a lot making reports and presentations. Thanks for all the help dude.

The most unforgettable moment with this guy would be the night before BTP viva II. Both of us didn;t do anything but somehow managed to make an awesome presentation with all flashy effects(that how we convinced Ramu everytime ).
