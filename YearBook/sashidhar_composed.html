For Himank Sharma 
Remember the first day and how the registration went? It all began there with me, Tejaswi, PK and Himank standing right behind each other. Himank was the guy who bitched the most and cursed the administration for taking things so slow. His popularity in first year can be seen from the fact that he (with Rama Reddy) was the bakra of section B. Any anything happened and he would be given bumps for no reason at all One must also not miss out on the hair style he had during those days. If you ever get the pics of independence day celebrations of 2005, do check out his hairstyle. Totally hilarious An honorary mention should also be made regarding his love for Preity Zinta in first year. He had this huge collection of Preity Zinta wallpapers which he used to so shamelessly flaunt

He loves doing BC, talking about stuff around. He realized it in first yr. itself that he can do BC with at max 4-5 people in the wing but if he uses a blog, he can reach out to a wider set of audience and thus started his blogging days. He is one of the most prolific bloggers and his name is sure to come first if history textbooks ever talk about blogging + UG2k5.

Throughout these four years he has taught me loads of stuff and for this reason becomes my guru. He taught me the right way to drink beer, smoke cigarretes and take neat vodka shots. Introduced me to the world of Bob Dylan and Elvis Presley. He is also pretty cool and handles difficult situations pretty well - one more thing I learnt. This list just goes on endlessly and I'm sure many others also regard him as a guru

We were in great contact when he was in NBH and me in GHEb and then from 3rd year onwards when we became "adjacent-wing" mates, we've known each other so much more. Tomorrow when he goes away I'll really miss his occasional BC, his jokes, his sarcasms, but most of all - him. Here's wishing him best of luck in whatever activity he takes up.

most memorable moment - I, Raman and Himank drinking beer on the road. Police comes and catches us.

For Deepti 
Apart from the usual exchanges of hi and hello, we never really talked to each other till the end of second year. That's when I, Maruti and Deepti were assigned project mates. At that time, all I knew about deepti was that she is this gal totally into studies and all. Our relation was on a very impersonal level and it continued to be so before changing to the bc stuff and all. Hell! I'll never know when the transition took place. Maybe the entire Felicity video making experience was a big catalyst but there were other things as well. Soon our discussion would be more of celebrity gossip, TV shows, news, etc. and less of project work. During the entire project episode, I came to know how very systematic she is, meticulously planning everything and making clearcut agendas and plans. A point worthy of mention here is that she can always be trusted for taking up responsibilities, whether it is organising some b'day party or getting people to come for a meeting.

I often use this technique of putting myself in the shoes of so and so person and thinking from his/her point of view. But in case of deepti, it has always been difficult for me because she is so radically different from me. I'm a born slacker. She is a born push-er and has it in her to push an idea and make sure it turns from something abstract (on paper or pdf or ppt) to something solid. She loves taking charge of things around her, being (a wee bit) bossy and all. Wait a minute! Did I say a wee bit bossy? She is so totally bossy!! Add to it her frankness. She will definitely give you her peice of mind if you don't work by her standards Plus her fundaes. She has one for every thing, ranging from weight loss to fashion tips, basically anything.
